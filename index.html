What is PowerBI?

Power BI is the leading business intelligence (BI) platform developed by Microsoft. It's a collection of tools that allows us to connect to various data sources, analyse the data, and then present that analysis in a visually appealing, interactive and user-friendly way.

It promotes collaboration by allowing users to easily share reports and dashboards with colleagues. This improves communication and decision-making by ensuring everyone has access to the same data-driven insights. Reports and dashboards can also be accessed from anywhere on any device, making it a great tool for remote teams.
